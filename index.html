UberTruck

Overview

UberTruck is a mobile application that links two parties i.e the truck drivers and its users. It's users are able to access all the details concerning the truck driver, the truck and the type of service he can deliver. The truck driver is usually given a rating by the user after delivery of service thus the drivers ensure they provide the best service to their customers. The aim of the application is for easier access of truck services in the country.
